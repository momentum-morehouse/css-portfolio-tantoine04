Garden Supply Store

Lazy River Nursery and Garden Supply has been serving the garden needs of the Tri-State area since 1976. We have supplies for everyone from the small hobby gardener to landscaping contractors.

At Lazy River, our staff are knowledgeable about horticulture and can give you expert advice. We have very low turnover, so you can feel confident knowing that we'll remember your gardening needs and can help you manage your garden for years to come
